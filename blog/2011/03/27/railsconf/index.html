RailsConf 2011

Mar 27th, 2011

Apparently RailsConf was held in Baltimore, MD last year and will be again this year. RailsConf 2011 will be held from May 16 - May 19 at the Baltimore Convention Center. I’m planning on attending along with at least one of my friends from the area. I’m pretty stoked to check out the Rails community, hear some great talks and code with some peeps.

Also be sure to checkout BohConf, the Official Unconf of RailsConf, and Ignite RailsConf 2. BohConf is free and Ignite RailsConf is ~$7 (both of these events are being organized by smartlogic).

On a side note, I’m playing around with Rails, Sinatra and some JavaScript via Backbone.js.
